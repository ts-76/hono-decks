# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hono and Cloudflare developers evaluating how to compile, serve, present, and export MDX slide decks from a Worker application.

## Product Purpose

Provide a runnable reference application that demonstrates the public `@hono/decks` integration surface and makes each route and capability easy to inspect.

## Brand Personality

Precise, lightweight, restrained.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page theatrics, decorative effects that compete with deck content, unfamiliar controls, and low-contrast neutral styling.

## Design Principles

- Keep the slide as the primary visual focus.
- Make library behavior and route boundaries easy to inspect.
- Prefer familiar controls and quiet supporting surfaces.
- Preserve deck-owned themes instead of styling slide content from the app shell.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, retain visible keyboard focus, respect reduced-motion preferences, and do not rely on color alone to communicate state.
